Welcome

Thank you for agreeing to participate in our study.

This study examines how people learn from investment decisions made by others. To this end, you will be shown information cards, one at a time. On each card you will see an amount of investment a person made and the outcome that resulted from this investment.

Please pay attention to the numbers. Your task is to evaluate the relationship between investment amounts and their outcomes, that is, the investment/outcome contingency.

After seeing 36 cards, we shall ask for your assessment of the investment/outcome contingency.

PLEASE NOTE:

It is important that you read the cards carefully. We will stop the presentation occasionally and ask you to report the investment or outcome you have just seen. Your submission will be voided in case you misreport that value twice.
